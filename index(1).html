Hi! I'm Eunyoung Kim, but you can call me Renée. I'm a PhD researcher in Educational Technology at Ewha Womans University, working with Prof. Hyo-Jeong So at the Interaction Design for Learning (IDL) Lab.

My research focuses on designing AI-based scaffolding systems that detect and respond to learners' cognitive and affective states in real time — particularly in simulation-based and complex case learning environments. I'm especially interested in when and how AI should intervene to support learners' judgment and reflection without replacing it.

I also serve as a Project Researcher at the National Cancer Center's Integrated Cancer Survivorship Support Center, where I develop educational programs for healthcare professionals.

Outside the lab, I'm a runner 🏃‍♀️ — recently completed my first 10K and working on beating my time. I also love reading widely and distilling insights across fields. If you're interested in academic collaboration or just want to chat about EdTech and AI, feel free to reach out!
