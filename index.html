Hi, I'm Eunyoung Kim (Rénee), a Ph.D. student in Educational Technology at Ewha Womans University, advised by Prof. Hyo-Jeong So at the Interaction Design for Learning Lab (IDL Lab).

My research begins with a fundamental question: what does it mean to learn in complex, AI-mediated environments? I examine how learners develop judgment, reflection, and expertise, and translate this understanding into the design of AI-supported learning environments. I am particularly interested in when, how, and in what role AI should intervene to support human learning without replacing human thinking.

I also bring practice-based experience in healthcare professional education through my work at the National Cancer Center.

Outside the lab, I'm a runner 🏃‍♀️ — recently completed my first 10K and always working on beating my time. I also love reading widely across fields and distilling insights.
